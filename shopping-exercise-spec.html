The ⚡ pill — shared pattern
Same visual, different meaning, same colour ramp
Both tabs use a ⚡ lightning bolt pill to express effort/intensity. Shopping: ⚡ Easy (green), ⚡ Medium (yellow), ⚡ Big cook (red). Exercise: ⚡ Light (green), ⚡ Moderate (yellow), ⚡ High (orange), ⚡ Peak (red). Same green→red colour ramp, same pill format, same position in the pill row. You can scan either tab and instantly read the effort without thinking about which tab you're on.
Shopping-specific pills
Cuisine · Carb · Richness
Cuisine — Italian (tomato red), Asian (orange), Mexican (flame), Greek (teal), BBQ (brown), Comfort (purple). Coloured by flavour region.

Carb type — Rice, Pasta, Noodles, Bread, Wraps, None. Neutral grey surface pill.

Richness — Light (green), Moderate (yellow), Rich (red). Calorie density of the meal.
Exercise-specific pills
Category(s) · Calories
Category type — Flex (teal), Cardio (orange), Strength (red), Core (pink). One per session, so dual days show two.

Calories — Combined total for the day. Uses green/amber/red colouring based on burn level. HealthKit actuals overwrite estimates after completion.

What the AI Briefing Does

With metadata on both tabs, the briefing makes cross-domain connections between food and exercise.
Shopping patterns
Variety & balance
"Three Italian meals Mon-Wed — the Thai curry on Thursday breaks the pattern nicely."

"Two big cooks back to back (Wed slow lamb + Sat BBQ) — Thursday and Friday are quick ones to give you a break."

"Light meals Mon and Fri, rich on Wed and Sat — good balance."
Exercise patterns
Load & recovery
"Peak day Thursday with the 10k — Friday's Yin Yoga + Core is a smart recovery."

"All cardio this week — Tuesday has room for a strength session."

"Three peak days Wed–Sat is a lot. Listen to your body Saturday."
Cross-domain intelligence
Food ↔ Exercise connections
"Heavy exercise Thursday (620 cal burn on the 10k) — good that Wednesday's slow lamb will fuel it." The AI reads the ⚡ effort/intensity across both tabs and makes connections. Peak exercise days pair with rich meals. Light exercise days pair with lighter food. Recovery days suggest easy cooking. This is the intelligence that makes the app more than three separate tabs.
